About Kid Mercury

Based in Wales, Kid Mercury is an alternative indie rock duo that merges catchy bright guitar riffs and smooth warm bass-lines with elements of alternative indie rock and jazz.

The group was formed by the two members Fraser Gully (vocalist/guitarist) and Freddy Hathaway (bassist), after their first year of University and during the Coronavirus pandemic. Because of this, their music is written, recorded and produced in their bedrooms.
